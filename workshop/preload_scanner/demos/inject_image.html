Injecting Elements and Preload Scanner

The demo shows that dynamically injecting elements, like images via JavaScript, won't be pre-loaded in parallel by the preload scanner.

Implications:

Dynamically injected images are not detected by the preload scanner and are not included in the parallel loading process.

Considerations:

Developers should carefully balance dynamic element injection and pre-loading strategies for optimal performance.
